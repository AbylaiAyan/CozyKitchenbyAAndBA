Eating right is not only healthy, but also delicious

Recently, the situation in the world has changed globally. Because of this, the health of most people has weakened. Now people pay great attention to health.

In addition to physical exercise, nutrition has a special role in the path to bringing and preserving the body in a healthy form. Therefore, do not forget that you need to eat a full and varied diet to keep your figure in good shape and strengthen your health and immunity.

Everyone knows that sausage, sausages, semi-finished products, cookies and sweets are junk food. But few people think that it is also very expensive. Cooking other dishes based on seasonings, salt, sugar and butter is also a harmful food. In order to save money and make stocks, many people buy semi-finished products that are stored for a long time. But all these products cause severe damage to the immune system and the figure.

For our users, we have created a special section so that they can find useful recipes based only on healthy and delicious products.

Products that can be consumed without restriction

  • lettuce, greens, spinach
  • tea, coffee without sugar and cream
  • non-carbonated drinking water
  • cabbage, mushrooms, cucumbers, tomatoes, peppers
  • meat
  • wild fish
  • zucchini, eggplant, beetroot, carrot, string beans
  • radish, radish, turnip, green peas

Products that need to be excluded or restricted as much as possible

  • vegetable oil
  • mayonnaise
  • exclude meat grown using hormones and antibiotics, usually pork and chicken
  • sugar, honey
  • canned meat, fish
  • cakes, cakes and other confectionery products
  • ice cream
  • carbonated and sugary drinks
  • cookies, pastry products
  • sweets, chocolate (chocolate in large quantities)
  • fruits and berries: bananas, grapes, dates, watermelon, melon
  • jam, jams
  • smoked meats, sausages, sausages
  • alcoholic beverages

It is important to know

  • the diagnosis of obesity is made when the waist size in men is more than 94 cm, in women - more than 80 cm with any height. Correctly measure at the navel level
  • for weight loss, 80% is proper nutrition, 20% is physical activity
  • physical activity can be started with walking for 30 minutes 3 times a week, gradually increasing the frequency and intensity of training (target speed – 6 km / hour, daily walk 3 kilometers in 30 minutes)good intensity is considered. Cycling, swimming, running, game sports: volleyball, football, basketball, tennis also helps well. In good weather, it is preferable to practice outdoors, in the morning, while there is strength and the air is still not heavily polluted with exhaust gases
  • the amount of liquid consumed is 30 ml /kg in healthy people, 40 ml/kg in obese people, but no more than 4 liters per day. Try to drink alkaline still water, the pH of which is approaching 7. The alkaline or acidic state of the water can be determined by special test strips for the oral cavity, which are sold in pharmacies. Here are some examples of the pH of water measured by us: Elbrussia - 7.0; Aqua Dombay - 6.5; Pilgrim - 4.5; springs of the Caucasus - 4.5; Mountain Peak - 4.5
  • nutrition should be varied and balanced. Of protein products, preference is given to meat of red fatty varieties (beef, lamb), fish (preferably wild, not farmed, which is grown outside the will), eggs, fermented dairy products. Completely exclude fast carbohydrates (sweets, sugary drinks, white flour products). Replenish carbohydrates at the expense of vegetables and fruits, preferably fresh
  • go to bed no later than 22.30, sleep duration is at least 7-8 hours. The sleep hormone melatonin is a fat burner. It is necessary to sleep in a well-ventilated room with fresh air, at a temperature of +19 degrees
