The best baking recipes

Baking is usually called products made of any kind of dough, cooked in an oven or oven. However, dough fried in a frying pan or in deep oil is also often called baking.

All pastries can be divided into sweet and unsweetened, and within each group there are many types of pastries: pies and pies, cupcakes, cookies, cakes and pastries, buns and cheesecakes from different types of dough. Homemade bread and all bakery products are also baked goods.

Traditional, unique pastries are available in any national cuisine. What can I say, any hostess will have more than one recipe for her favorite sweet and unsweetened pastries. So we can safely say that there are not even hundreds of baking recipes, but thousands!

For baking to be successful, you need a well-adjusted gas or electric oven. But its absence is not at all a reason to abandon homemade baking: a microwave oven with an oven function, a mini oven, an aerogrill and even a slow cooker will perfectly cope with the preparation of many types of pastries.

It is best to measure the ingredients for making homemade cakes using kitchen scales, but if there are none, a measuring cup will do.

Homemade cakes are one of the symbols of a cozy home. Have a good baking!

Quick Napoleon Cake
Quick cake "Napoleon" from the finished puff pastry is not too difficult to prepare even for a novice cook, because, in fact, his task is to create a cream. Well, even a child can bake cakes from the existing base! However, the process of making the cream for this cake will require your attention, patience, and a certain skill. The most important thing is not to go far from the stove while the mass is cooking: you were distracted for just a minute, and the cream has already burned. If you do everything right, then such a delicious Napoleon cake will appear on the table that no one will even think of asking you if you prepared the dough yourself or used the finished puff pastry.
Step 1
Put the frozen puff pastry for the Napoleon cake out of the package on the desktop. Let it thaw completely at room temperature. Heat the oven to 200°C.
Step 2
Line the baking tray for baking Napoleon cake cakes with baking paper. Divide the thawed puff pastry into two parts and roll out into layers about 5 mm thick.
Step 3
Cut the edges of the layers so that you get squares with a side of 22 cm. Transfer one of them to a baking sheet. Prick it often with a fork so that it does not swell when baking. Place the dough scraps side by side.
Step 4
Place a baking sheet with a layer of dough in the oven for 10-12 minutes. Transfer the finished Napoleon cake cake to the grill and let it cool completely. On the same baking sheet, bake the second cake and the dough trimmings. Let it cool down.
Step 5
Prepare the cream for the Napoleon cake. Pour the starch into a ladle. Add 30 g of sugar and mix. Pour in the milk. Add the seeds from the vanilla pod, stir and put on fire. Stirring constantly with a whisk, bring the mixture almost to a boil.
Step 6
In a separate bowl, whisk the yolks with the remaining sugar. Continuing to beat, pour in the milk mixture in a thin stream. Pour into a ladle again, put on fire and bring to a boil, stirring continuously. Cook for 2 minutes.
Step 7
Pour the Napoleon cake cream into a clean bowl, add the butter and mix. Allow to cool completely at room temperature. Chop the baked dough scraps with a rolling pin.
Step 8
Spread the bottom cake with two thirds of the cream. Cover with a second cake, brush with the remaining cream and sprinkle with the resulting crumbs. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving.
Lean pie with plums and cranberries
A lean pie with plums and lingonberries is not necessary to eat only during lent. It can be baked both for vegans and for those who want to give up animal protein for a while. If the recipe is good, the lean pie is not inferior to the taste of the most familiar, the dough for which is prepared with eggs and butter.
Step 1
Put the vegetable oil in the freezer for about 30 minutes. It should thicken. Step 2
Fill the cranberries and plums with sugar, put on medium heat, pour in 0.5 cups of water, close the lid and bring to a boil. Remove the lid and cook for 3 minutes. Pour the syrup into a bowl, cool.
Step 3
Sift the flour with salt and baking powder into a bowl. Lay out the frozen butter, chop the dough with two knives to make a crumb. Pour in the syrup, add vanilla sugar and cinnamon, knead the dough quickly.
Step 4
Sprinkle a silicone baking dish with a diameter of 22 cm with water. Put a little dough on the bottom, distribute the plum and lingonberries evenly.
Step 5
Put the remaining dough on top, put the mold in the center of the oven. Bake at 180 ° C for 35-40 minutes. A wooden skewer stuck in the center of the pie should come out dry.
Step 6
Cool the pie on the grill, carefully turn it onto a platter, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve.
Simple raspberry pie "Raspberry jingle"
Even a novice hostess can easily cope with the preparation of such a simple raspberry pie. Well, for an experienced one, there will be no problems at all: just a solid pleasure from the ease of the process and the inimitable berry flavor of the filling! Such a pie can be cooked at least every day, especially at the cottage in the raspberry season. Just like that, I got up in the morning, filled a bowl with berries from a bush and half the business is in the hat. It remains only to make the dough according to the recipe and wait for the moment when a simple raspberry pie is ready (spoiler: a little more than half an hour). What's next? And then set the table, boil the kettle, take out the dishes. And be ready for the visit of neighbors, who will certainly be "invited" to visit the magical aroma of berry pastries!
Step 1
Add butter and yogurt to the beaten eggs Prepare the dough for a simple raspberry pie. Beat the eggs until fluffy. Pour in the sugar, continuing to beat until the mass doubles. Then add the melted butter and yogurt (with soda).
Step 2
Preparing the dough for raspberry pie Sift the flour through a fine sieve into a separate bowl. Then pour into the egg mass and beat well with a whisk again. The dough should not be too thick.
Step 3
Pour the dough into the mold and decorate with berries Grease a baking dish for a simple raspberry pie with a small amount of butter. Pour out half of the prepared dough, spread the berries on top in one layer.
Step 4
Pour the remaining raspberry dough. Carefully smooth out with a silicone spatula. Put the remaining raspberries. Heat the oven to 180 °C. Place the mold with a simple raspberry pie on the middle level.
Step 5
Bake in a hot oven for 35 minutes. Let the finished cake cool in the mold until warm. It is better to serve it on the table, because the pie turns out to be very tender. Sprinkle with vanilla powdered sugar.
Step 6
Cool the pie on the grill, carefully turn it onto a platter, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve.
Coconut cake with caramel and banana

Coconut pie shifter with creamy caramel and banana will be a perfect end to a family dinner.
Step 1
Prepare the products. This quantity is designed for a form of 10x20 cm.
Step 2
For the dough, beat 110 grams of soft butter with 140 grams of sugar, continuing to beat, add eggs one at a time, pour 80 ml of milk. Combine the flour with baking powder and sift. Add the dry ingredients to the butter-egg mixture and pour in the coconut chips. Mix it up.
Step 3
Melt 40 grams of sugar in a saucepan. Add 40 grams of butter, stir the mixture with a silicone spatula until smooth, pour in 2 tablespoons of milk.
Step 4
The bottom of the mold is lined with parchment, spread out banana circles and pour caramel.
Step 5
Put the prepared dough on the caramel and distribute it according to the form. Bake the pie in the oven, preheated to 180 ° C, for 35 minutes. Turn the warm finished cake onto a platter.
